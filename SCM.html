9. Supply Chain Management
This section lists the chapter formulas only. Use the Enhanced General Calculator above for arithmetic, powers, and square roots, and use the Statistical Tools above for z-scores and service levels.

Chapter 9 Formulas

Stockout Probability = Number of stockouts / Total items
In-Stock Probability = 1 − Stockout Probability
On-Order Inventory = Lead Time × Mean demand
On-Hand Inventory = √(L + 1) × Standard Deviation × z
Holding Cost per Unit = h × (On-Order Inventory + On-Hand Inventory) / Mean demand

10. Forecasting
Simple Chapter 10 formulas are listed for the Enhanced General Calculator. More complicated Chapter 10 methods use input panels below.

Formula-Only Items

Forecast Error (FEt) = Forecast − Actual
Squared Error = (FEt)^2
Absolute Error = ABS(FEt)
MSE = Average of squared forecast errors
MAE = Average of absolute forecast errors
Naïve Forecast = Last realized value
Moving Average Forecast = Sum of last T realized values / T
Forecast Combination = Average of individual forecasts
